Hayshakers square dance lessons continue every Sunday from 5 until 7 p.m. with Mark Wheeler instructing in the Pacific Grange, 90475 Highway 101 in Warrenton near the Cullably Lake turnoff. The first 1½ hours are lessons, then Mainstream for the last ½ hour. The 4th Sunday will be a floor level dance for the entire 2 hours.
