<p>The Federation is pleased to announce that the 2015 Randall Award, the 
highest honor presented by the Oregon Federation, was presented at
Mid-Winter Festival to Paul and Dotty Swenson of the Tualatin Valley
Council.
<p>The Randall Award is preseted to a couple or individual who have
demonstrated a lifetime commitment to square dancing and to square
dancers, and the Swensons certainly meet those requirements. Our
congratulations!

<p>Oregon's 2015 Summer Festival will be held at the Klamath County Fairgrounds in 
Klamath Falls, July 17 to 19, 2015.
