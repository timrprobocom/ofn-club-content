Emerald Empire Area Council's annual Benefit Dance will be on SATURDAY, MARCH 28, 2015 at the Emerald Square Dance Center, 2095 Yolanda St., Springfield, OR.  Pre-rounds 7:30 p.m., and Mainstream with Plus at 8:00 p.m.  Calling and Cueing are being donated by the Cascade Callers and Cuers Association.
There will be a Silent Auction of many great items.  The recipient this year is the Willamette Valley Chapter of "HONOR FLIGHT", and besides World War II Veterans they are now also honoring Korean War Veterans.  Spectators welcome.  For information, contact Sharon at 541-731-3130.
