 The Hayshakers will hold its 68th Anniversary Dance on Friday May 15th from 7 p.m. until 10 p.m.  Mark Wheeler and Jim Hattrick will call and cue throughout the night.  Plus Squares will be danced from 7 until 8 p.m., then Mainstream from 8 until 10 with round dances alternating during both.  Refreshments will be served at 10.  The dance will be held in the Pacific Grange which is located at 90475 Highway 101 midway between Seaside and Astoria.
  Craig Holt
