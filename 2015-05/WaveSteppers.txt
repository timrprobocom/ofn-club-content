The Wave Steppers' square dance classes, taught by club caller Joe Wrabek, continue every Wednesday evening at the Tillamook Elks Lodge, 1907 Third St. in downtown Tillamook.  The classes are free.
 The club's first dance of the season, Saturday May 9th, will be A New Dancers Dance, with caller Joe Wrabek and line dance instructor Gwen Kiel.
 Saturday, June 13 is the club's famous Elvis Dance, with caller Mark Wheeler as Elvis, and round dancing with cuer Connie Clark.
 The Wave Steppers will hold a Patriotic Dance on Saturday, July 11, with George Clark calling and Cueing.  Our Hawaiian Dance Saturday, August 8, will feature Harvey Hunsucker as caller and cuer.
 The club's anniversary is in September; Joe Wrabek will call the Anniversary Dance, with Gwen Kiel conducting line dancing.  Joe and Gwen will team up again for the Fall Dance on Saturday, October 10.
 All the club's dances are held the second Saturday of the month, on the Dance Floor at Garibaldi City Hall, 107 6th St. (one block off US 101).  Most of the second floor of the 1948 vintage building is a dance floor, built for the old Firemen's Balls.
  Joe Wrabek
