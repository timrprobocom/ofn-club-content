2019 Federation Officer Nominees

Here are the candidates running for office for the 2019-2020 year in the Oregon Federation of Square and Round Dance Clubs. Your club secretary will receive a ballot by early March, and the ballot is due back with the Federation Secretary by April 21.

The current President (Lorri McIntosh) automatically becomes the Past President. The current 1st Vice President (Karyn Buchheit) automatically becomes the President.
