Now that spring has begun, I am sure Oregon will soon be back to the
sun-filled days to which we Oregonians we are all accustomed.
<p>
This month, we have a letter reminding us not to get stuck in a
rut, and an obituary for long-time caller Rick Ewing.
<p>
Just before press time, we received word that the Federation has
received a very generous donation from the estate of long time
dance Susan Corbett, who lost her fight with cancer some time
ago.  The Federation board will be discussing the best way to
use these funds for the benefit of Oregon's square dance
community.
