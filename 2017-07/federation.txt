<h3>Publicity</h3>
<p>Summer time brings many opportunities to travel around the state, visiting festivals and special dances allowing you the pleasure of renewing old friendships and to make new friends.</p>
<p>Oregon&rsquo;s Summer Festival in Salem at the Blanchet Catholic School (4373 Market Street NE, Salem) will start their pre-festival kick off on Thursday, July 13th with a free tour of the Oregon&rsquo;s State Capitol.&nbsp; Meet at the Blanchet School at 1:00 PM to carpool to the Capitol. The Trail's End Dance that evening will be at the Salem Square Dance Center (3695 45th Avenue NE, Salem) with the Wild Cards. Summer Festival starts on Friday, July 14th with the unique and fun opportunity to dance on the steps of the Capitol (900 Court St. N.E.) from 2:30-4:00 PM with the Crew.&nbsp; Dancing will resume at the Blanchet Catholic School at 6:00 pm.&nbsp; This year&rsquo;s Summer Festival&rsquo;s featured caller is Johnny Preston and featured cuers are Rey &amp; Sherry Garza.&nbsp; Many of our own great callers and cuers will also be on the festival program. Check out the program schedule to find out when your favorite callers and cuers will be on the stage.</p>
<p>On Saturday, July 15, in addition to the many dancing opportunities, there will also be a special educational seminar.&nbsp; JoAnn MacDonald from the Consumer Protection and Financial Fraud section of the Oregon Department of Justice will be leading a seminar on how to recognize and protect yourself against a scam or consumer fraud, and what to do if you suspect a scam or fall victim to a scam.&nbsp; Please bring your questions and be prepared for a very informative and enlightening session.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Daryl Clendenin will also be leading a sack lunch panel discussion at noon on Saturday to discuss the future of square dancing.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Summer Festival&rsquo;s two final events will be on Sunday, July 16.&nbsp; Our Oregon Federation State Meeting will start at 9:30 PM.&nbsp; We welcome visitors and hope you decide to come and watch the meeting.&nbsp; The Summer Festival&rsquo;s Trail's Out Dance will be from 11:00-1:00 PM in the Mainstream Hall with Johnny Preston and Rey &amp; Sherry Garza.</p>
<p>Summer Festival Ribbons are available from committee members, by mail, or at the door.&nbsp; Hope to see most of you there.&nbsp; If you have children who could use financial assistance to purchase their ribbons or for a list of the many youth activities that are scheduled for the weekend, please contact the Federation&rsquo;s Youth Activities Coordinator, Karyn Buchheit (<a href="mailto:buchheitfarmkm@gmail.com">buchheitfarmkm@gmail.com</a>).</p>
<p>Kathy Roberts<br />Education and Publicity Chairman</p>
