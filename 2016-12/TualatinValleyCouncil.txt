The next TVC event will be our annual New Years Eve dance at the IOOF Hall, 267 East Main Street in Hillsboro.  The caller and cuer for the evening will be Daryl and Yvonne Clendenin.  There is a new format this year.  The dance begins at 9:00 PM and goes until 12:15 AM.  Mid dance there will be a break where desserts and coffee will be available.  At midnight there will be a pause in the dancing to usher in the New Year.
