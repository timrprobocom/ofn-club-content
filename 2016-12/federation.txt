<h3>Mid-Winter</h3>
<p>&ldquo;Shootin&rsquo; for the Moon&rdquo;&nbsp; Mid- Winter Festival 2017, Linn County Fairgrounds in Albany, January 27 to 29, 2017. <a href="http://midwinterfestival.com">Click here</a> for forms and more information.&nbsp;</p>
<div>Now is the time to purchase your mid-winter festival ribbon. The National callers this year will be Mike Seastrom and Hunter Keller, Sharon &amp; Casey Parker will be the cuers, and the clogging&nbsp;instructor is Barry Welch, all are from California. IN addition, your favorite Oregon callers will make this a great week-end of dancing. &nbsp;A bus is available to take you from the Fair grounds to the restaurants or to the mall. &nbsp;Friday at 11am, there will be a Dance Demo at the Heritage Mall, with Leonard Snodgrass as the caller.</div>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<div>You can shop at the fair grounds in the Vendors hall or at Resale Clothing for some great bargains.&nbsp; The Sewing Clinic has been moved to the Holliday Inn.&nbsp; The program books will have information on dances, sewing clinic demos, where to eat, and much more.&nbsp; The clogging will be at the Comfort Inn. &nbsp;They are really fun to watch.&nbsp;</div>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<div>See you in Albany, January 27-29, 2017 at the Mid Winter Festival.</div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div>Marie Dorsey</div>
