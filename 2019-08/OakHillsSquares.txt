We dance on Monday evenings at the Oak Hills Recreation Center on NW 153rd and Oak Hills Drive (2400 NW 153rd Avenue, Beaverton).  6:30 to 7:30 are the lessons and 7:35 to 8:30 is Mainstream dancing.  On the first Monday of each month the second hour is a dance with all singing calls.  Lane Clem is our club caller.
