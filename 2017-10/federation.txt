<h3>Federation Meeting</h3>
<p>The Federation executive board held its quarterly meeting on September 17 at the Kinton Graonge in Beaverton. &nbsp;At this meeting, your new officers for the 2017-2018 square dance year were installed:</p>
<ul>
<li>Kathy Roberts, Tualatin Valley Council, President</li>
<li>Lorri McIntosh, Rogue-Sis-Q Council, 1st Vice President</li>
<li>Bob Houston, South Coast Council, 2nd Vice President</li>
<li>Tim Hagey, Emerald Empire Council, Secretary</li>
<li>Lane Clem, Tualatin Valley Council, Treasurer</li>
<li>Patty Cooper, South Coast Council, Membership</li>
<li>Marilyn Schmit, Mid-Willamette Council, Past President</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition, Dale and Kathy Worthington were named the newest Goodwill Ambassadors for the state. &nbsp;We congratulate all of these volunteers and thank them for their service to Oregon's square dance community.</p>
<p>In meeting business, the Federation&nbsp;voted to establish a process to get background checks on all adults involved with youth at Federation-sponsored events. &nbsp;Dave Cooper pointed out that, in future years, you might not remember who called or who cued, but you'll remember the special things that made a dance or festival fun. &nbsp;He is going to be writing more about adding fun into our activity in upcoming months.</p>
