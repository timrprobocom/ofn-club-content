<h3>Mid-Winter</h3>
<p>Caller: Mike Seastrom has been square dancing since 1960 and calling since 1963. He taught his first class in 1963 after borrowing money from his folks to buy a small sound system and was able to pay them back before the class was over.After receiving his doctorate in 1976, Mike joined caller CALLERLAB in 1977 and has been chairman of several committees. &nbsp;Mike teaches Basic through C-1, prompts contras and also enjoys doing dance party events when his schedule allows. Calling gave Mike the opportunity to travel in the United States and many other country. He enjoys dancing as well as calling. Mike is also a practicing dentist.</p>
<p>Caller:</p>
<p>Hunter Keller has been around square dancing all his life, having been dragged from dance to dance by his grandparents and parents.He met and became friends with many national callers with whom h is still friends today. After the death of his grandfather, in 2002, Hunter decided that he wanted to do something that would always remind him of his grandfather and decided to take square dance lessons in 2004 and six months later he picked up a microphone and began calling.Hunter calls for four clubs. He Called his first festival in July 2007 in California. He also has called in many different country. Hunter has been staff on square dance land and cruise vacations. Hunter is a member of callerlab and&nbsp; Ways and Means Committee. Hunter Credits almost everything he has learned throughout the past 15 years to 4-H, and says that if it were not for that program, he would not be where he is today.</p>
<p>Cuer:</p>
<p>Sharon and Casey Parker met at a square dance beginners hoe-down in January of 1971. Sharon began cueing and teaching rounds in 1975 while at the same time Casey began calling squares. Sharon currently has workshops covering phases 1-VI. They have written many round dances and have been featured Round Dance Instructors at many festivals throughout the United States. They were on staff at the McCloud Dance County Square &amp; Round Dance Vacationland in Northern California for over 25 years, with their own week long dance and new/beginning teacher clinic. They have taught at many State and National Conventions and have been clinicians at several ROUNDALAB Conventions. They are charter members of ROUNDALAB and have served on both the Board of Directors and Executive committee of ROUNDALAB.</p>
<p>Clogging Instructor:</p>
<p>Berry Welch has been teaching clogging for 34 years and have taught work shops in California, Oregon, Washington, Arizona, Nevada, Texas, Missouri, Oklahoma, Tennessee, Kentucky,New York, Massachusetts, Main, North Carolina, Florida,Indiana, Maryland, Georgia, Arkansas, Wisconsin, as well as Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.&nbsp; I teach a class each week at home in Fresno, California and have a bunch of crazy people that seem to enjoy themselves. I teach all levels from beginner through advanced.&nbsp; I have judged numerous competitions over the years and I have made a lot of great friends!&nbsp;</p>
<p>Mid Winter-Festival 2017 is Just a few weeks away, every thing is ready. Come to Albany for a really fun festival, on January 27, 28 &amp;29 2017.&nbsp;</p>
<div>Marie Dorsey</div>
