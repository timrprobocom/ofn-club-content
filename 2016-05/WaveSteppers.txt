The Wave Steppers’ “Spring Fling” Dance Saturday, May 14th and “Western Dance” Saturday, June 11th will be called by Club caller Joe Wrabek, with line dancing by Club line dance instructor Gwen Kiel.  The club’s dances are on the second Saturday of the month, on the Dance Floor at Garibaldi City Hall, 107 Sixth St. (at Acacia) in downtown Garibaldi.  The city of Garibaldi is 9 miles north of Tillamook on US 101.  The dances all start at 7 p.m.
 The Club’s “Beach Dance” on Saturday, July 9th will have Harvey Hunsucker as caller and cuer. The “County Fair Dance,” which happens the last day of the Tillamook County Fair, Saturday, August 13th) will have caller Darrell Kalmbach and cuer Connie Clark; Connie will also be cueing at the “Elvis Dance” Saturday, Sept. 10th, with caller Mark Wheeler as Elvis.  And the last dance of the 2016 season -- &rdquo;a “Fall Dance” Saturday, Oct. 8th&rdquo; -- will have club caller Joe Wrabek and line dancing with Gwen Kiel.
 The Wave Steppers’ free square dance classes continue on Wednesdays, 6 to 8 p.m. at the Tillamook Elks Lodge, 1907 Third St. in downtown Tillamook.  The club has also been providing angels to the Hayshakers’ square dance classes in Warrenton on the first and third Fridays.
  Joe Wrabek
