Emerald Empire Area Council's annual Benefit Dance this year will be on Saturday evening, April 30 at the Emerald Dance Center, 2095 Yolanda St., Springfield.  Callers will be;  K.C. Curtis, Darrell Kalmbach, and
George Hermann.  Cuers are;  Christina Corelli, Jackie Gale, and Patty Hermann.  Pre-rounds at 7:30 and squares at 8:00, mainstream level.  There will be many "Silent Auction" items, and special 'Door Prizes'.  The recipient this year is "Honor Flight" where veterans of World War II and the Korean War receive a trip to Washington D.C. to be honored at the Memorials there.
