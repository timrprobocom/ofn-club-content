On July 12th R Square D's Denim Dance was called by Harvey Hunsucker and ice cream sundaes were enjoyed by all at break.  On July 26th Mike Stout called our hot summer night dance, and it was a hot summer night.  The dance was followed by Root Beer Floats.  Lonnie Sycks cued both of these dances.  Julie Stout was a guest cuer on the 26th.  Thank you Julie!
 In September R Square D reverts back to our winter dance schedule which is the second Friday and fourth Saturday.  Plus dancing begins at 7 and mainstream at 8PM.
 October brings Octoberfest.  It will be held the 2nd weekend in October, 13th to 15th, at the Rose Valley Grange Hall.  Several of our members have been working with Grange members to improve the acoustics in the hall.  Adam Christman and Craig Abercrombie will be calling and Susan Healea will be cueing.
 Back to fall and lessons.  Lessons begin October 18th.  
 For more information about us please visit our website at www.r-square-d.info or friend us on Facebook.  Our Caller for R Square D is Craig Abercrombie and our cuer is Lonnie Sycks.
