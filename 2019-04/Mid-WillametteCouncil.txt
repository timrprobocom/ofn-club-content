Alzheimer’s Research Fund Raiser Dance: The Mid-Willamette Area is sponsoring a special dance to raise funds for Alzheimer’s research on March 24 at 1:30 p.m. at the Salem Square Dance Center. Caller and Cuer MC will be Bruce Lowther and Judy Russell. Featured callers and cuers will be KC Curtis, Christina Corelli, George and Patty Herman.  All of the proceeds from the dance will go to OHSU to be used for research into Alzheimer’s.
