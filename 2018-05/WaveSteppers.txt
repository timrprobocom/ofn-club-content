The Wave Steppers’ Mother’s Day Dance will be Saturday, May 12th (the day before Mother’s Day) 7-9 p.m., on the Dance Floor at Garibaldi City Hall, with club caller Joe Wrabek and line dance coach Gwen Kiel.
 Garibaldi is nine miles north of Tillamook on US 101.  City Hall has a huge second-floor dance floor built for the old Firemen’s Balls.  The club has had square dances there since the building was built in 1948.
  Joe Wrabek
