The Hayshakers are dancing once again after two years.  We dance the 2nd & 4th Saturdays of every month from 7:00 until 9:00 p.m. with Mark Wheeler calling.  Mark teaches square dancing for 1½ hours from 7:00 until 8:30 and then a ½ hour Mainstream dance is held from 8:30 until 9 o’clock.
  Craig Holt
