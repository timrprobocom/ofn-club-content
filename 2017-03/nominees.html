2017 Federation Officer Nominees

Here are the candidates running for office for the 2017-2018 year in the Oregon Federation of Square and Round Dance Clubs. Your club secretary will receive a ballot by early March, and the ballot is due back with the Federation Secretary by May 1.

The current President (Marilyn Schmit) automatically becomes the Past President. The current 1st Vice President (Kathy Roberts) automatically becomes the President. The Treasurer is elected for a 2-year term, and his term is up this year.
