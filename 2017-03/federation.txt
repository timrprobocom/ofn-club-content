<h3>Publicity/Education Report</h3>
<p class="MsoNormal">On April 2, the Oregon Federation of Square and Round Dance Clubs and its Youth Activities Coordinator Karyn Buchheit will be hosting a Youth Educational Scholarship Benefit Dance at the Maplewood Grange. KC Curtis will be calling the dance with Tami Helms cueing. The benefit will start with a baked potato bar dinner at 4:00 pm, followed by a mainstream dance from 5:00-7:00 pm. During the dance, we will have a basket raffle and split the pot.&nbsp; We would like to request basket donations from all of the councils and clubs or individuals for the basket raffle.&nbsp; If you need any ideas on what type of items you might provide, please contact me at <a href="mailto:kathy@4roberts.us">kathy@4roberts.us</a>.&nbsp; All funds raised from this dance will be donated to the OFSRDC Youth Education Scholarship fund, providing scholarships for graduating high school seniors and college freshmen who are members of OFSRDC clubs and are planning on continuing their education. &nbsp;&nbsp;If you have any questions about the dance or would like to find out where to send monetary donations if you are unable to attend the dance, please contact Karyn Buchheit at <a href="mailto:buchheitfarmkm@gmail.com">buchheitfarmkm@gmail.com</a>.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">The 2017 Oregon Summer Festival will be held in Salem this year at the Blanchet Catholic School July 14-16.&nbsp; Johnny Preston is the featured &nbsp;caller.&nbsp; Featured cuers will be Rey &amp; Sherry Garza.&nbsp; If you haven&rsquo;t purchased your ribbons yet, please click on this link: <a href="http://www.squaredance.gen.or.us/content/SummerFestival2017.pdf">http://www.squaredance.gen.or.us/content/SummerFestival2017.pdf</a> to print out your registration form and send it in the address on the form.&nbsp; Summer Festival has arranged many special activities that you will not want to miss including a tour of the capitol on Thursday and dancing on the steps of the capitol on Friday. There will also be a youth program during the weekend.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">If you haven&rsquo;t already joined our Federation&rsquo;s Facebook page, from your FB wall search for &ldquo;Oregon Federation and Square and Round Dance Clubs&rdquo; and click the JOIN button so you can post your club&rsquo;s flyers and your comments. &nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Kathy Roberts<br />Publicity/Education Chairman</p>
